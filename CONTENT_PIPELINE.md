# Content Pipeline

Inventory of all narrative content, encounter mappings, and asset requirements needed to ship the game.

## Covering:

- **Data Spool Inventory** — Complete list of every Data Spool in the game, organized by thread (The Last Voices, Official Histories, Graal Echoes, Silent Host, The Signal). Estimated word count per spool. Which wreck tiers/systems drop which spools. Rarity tiers (common, uncommon, rare, unique).

- **Cult Encounter Mapping** — Which systems/regions contain each of the 10 cults. What form the encounter takes (wreck, station, signal, environmental set piece). Required player level/region access. Signature find items and their narrative triggers.

- **Faction Wreck Variety** — Visual differentiation notes: what makes a Directorate ship look Directorate vs. Hegemony vs. Accord vs. independent. Decal sets, hull shape language, interior fitout differences.

- **Graal Encounter Design** — How Graal wrecks differ from human wrecks in processing mechanics, resource yields, and narrative delivery. Special mechanics (alien data translation, emotional memory playback).

- **Environmental Set Pieces** — Specific locations with bespoke visual storytelling: The Endless Launch control center, Dr. Venn's station, the Flipside server, The Redundancy AI facility, the Echo Pilgrim conversion station. Required assets, interaction points, narrative triggers.

- **Sound Design Brief** — Complete list of sound effects needed: scanner pings, extractor impacts, probe thrusters, UI clicks, ambient hums, Data Spool audio filters, Graal emotional audio, The Echo's resonant frequency. Musical direction — if any.

- **Visual Asset List** — Every sprite/model/screen needed: wreck cross-sections (8 tiers × multiple factions), system map backgrounds, CRT monitor frames, button/switch assets, upgrade icons, resource icons, faction decals, probe/extractor models.
